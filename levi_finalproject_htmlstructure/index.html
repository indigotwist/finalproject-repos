Add New Recipe

Many families have a cookbook, or a collection of recipes, that have been passed down from generation to generation. These are more than just recipes. They are a family's story told through food and are just as precious as photo albums or other hierlooms.

Kooky Booky protects family recipes by collecting them into an easily shareable, printable, and lasting digital record. This allows aspiring family chefs to collaborate and add their favorite or most cherished recipes, collectively adding another chapter to the story.
